Service Agreement

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of this 1st day of January, 2025 ("Effective Date"), by and between ClientCo, a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of Delaware ("Client"), and TechSolutions, Inc., a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of New York ("Provider").


1. Scope of Services.

1.1 Provider agrees to perform the services ("Services") as described in the Statement of Work attached hereto as Exhibit A ("SOW"). Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to deliver the Services in a professional and workmanlike manner.

1.2 Any changes to the Services must be agreed upon in writing by both parties. No oral modifications to this Agreement shall be valid.


2. Payment and Fees.

2.1 Client shall pay Provider the fees set forth in the SOW. All invoices are due and payable within thirty (30) days of receipt. Late payments shall incur interest at a rate of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is less.


3. Termination.

3.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon thirty (30) days' prior written notice to the other party.

3.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice to the other party if the other party breaches a material provision of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within ten (10) days of receiving written notice of the breach.


4. Confidentiality.

4.1 All non-public information disclosed by one party to the other, whether orally or in writing, that is designated as confidential or that, by the nature of the information, would be reasonably understood to be confidential, shall be considered "Confidential Information." The receiving party agrees to use the same degree of care to protect Confidential Information as it uses to protect its own similar information, but in no event less than a reasonable degree of care.

Plain Language Summary

  • This is a *Service Agreement* between ClientCo (the customer) and TechSolutions (the service provider).
  • *What will be done?* The provider will perform specific services listed in a separate document called the "Statement of Work."
  • *Can the work change?* Any changes must be written down and agreed to by both companies.
  • *When do you pay?* You must pay within 30 days of getting an invoice. If you pay late, there will be a fee.
  • *Can the agreement be ended?* Either company can end the agreement with a 30-day written notice. Also, if one company breaks a major rule, the other can end the agreement immediately if the problem isn't fixed within 10 days.
  • *What about secrets?* Both companies must keep each other's private information confidential.
